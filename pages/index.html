What is QuickMVC?

QuickMVC is a simple framework designed to help you understand and implement the Model-View-Controller (MVC) concepts in web application development. With a focus on ease of use and speed, QuickMVC provides a clear structure and a solid foundation to kickstart your projects.

Simple Structure

With an organized folder structure, you can easily grasp how each part of the application functions.

Efficient Learning

Ideal for beginners and developers looking to refresh their understanding of MVC and OOP without unnecessary complexity.

Easy Customization

With a flexible architecture, you can quickly tailor and expand your applications to meet your needs.
